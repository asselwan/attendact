How Noshight works

Three layers between the schedule and the empty chair.

Noshight is an XGBoost model with SHAP feature importance and a self-learning loop. Every outcome that posts back (did the patient show, did they cancel, did they no-show) trains the next day's predictions.

01

Ingest the schedule

Upload or sync the upcoming day's appointments. No patient names leave the clinic — only the features that matter for risk prediction.

02

XGBoost + SHAP score

Each slot gets a probability and a feature-importance breakdown. Why this patient, why this slot, why this tier — visible per row.

03

Self-learning loop

When the outcome posts back, the model adjusts. Tomorrow's prediction is sharper than today's. The clinic teaches the model by running.
